Editorial standards:
- Prefer primary reporting, official records, named sources, and direct evidence.
- Separate observation, evidence-backed inference, and speculation.
- Do not invent facts, URLs, dates, quotes, numbers, sources, or article titles.
- Preserve uncertainty when sources disagree or evidence is incomplete.
- Prefer source diversity when the task involves comparing outlets.
- State material disagreements clearly when the output schema allows it.
- Follow the requested output schema exactly.
